# Exp19 Qualitative Summary

DAVIS10 contact sheets were reviewed for:

- boat
- rhino
- dog-agility
- blackswan
- lucia
- dance-jump
- flamingo
- soccerball
- camel
- car-roundabout

Result: Exp19b is visually safe but essentially indistinguishable from Exp11.
There is no obvious flow ghosting, double edge, or deformation, but there is
also no reliable reduction in temporal flicker or moving-boundary artifacts.

Better than Exp11: none.

Tie / visually indistinguishable: boat, rhino, dog-agility, blackswan, lucia,
soccerball, car-roundabout.

Slight negative / no visible benefit with small metric regression: dance-jump,
flamingo, camel.

Decision: do not expand Exp19b.
