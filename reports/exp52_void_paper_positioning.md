# Exp52 VOID Paper Positioning

VOID remains an audited VOR-OR baseline and same-model loser generator candidate. Exp52 strengthens the engineering story by showing that winner-preserving local objectives can reduce the loser-dominant DPO mechanism, but it does not establish a third adapter backbone.

Allowed statement: VOID official inference works on VOR-style inputs, and VOID-native preference wrapper mechanics can pass cache, row0, checkpoint, reload, and bounded one-step diagnostics.

Not allowed: VOID third-backbone positive, universal adapter, final SOTA, or all-models-supported claims.

DiffuEraser and VideoPainter remain the main positive adapter evidence. VOID remains high-risk adapter engineering until a heldout 10-step micro gate becomes positive/promising with visual support.
