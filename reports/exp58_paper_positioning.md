# Exp58 Paper Positioning

Exp58 should be described as a blocked native-data diagnostic, not as negative evidence against VOID.

Supported wording:

- VOID official inference and VOR-derived diagnostics work from prior experiments.
- VOR-derived adapter attempts repeatedly show transition-region damage.
- Official VOID-native data is the correct next diagnostic because VOID training data is generated counterfactual data, not VOR.
- Exp58 could not execute that native-data test because the official Kubric renderer environment is missing TensorFlow-compatible Kubric import and Blender/`bpy`.

Forbidden wording:

- Do not claim universal adapter.
- Do not claim final SOTA.
- Do not claim third-backbone evidence.
- Do not claim data mismatch is confirmed.
- Do not claim VOID adapter failure on native data.
